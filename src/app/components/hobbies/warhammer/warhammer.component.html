Warhammers


Since I was a kid I started collecting warhammers with my young brother after visiting a 'games workshop' near our house. I barely play though, just painted a lot of them until I grow older and didn't have time for it. Recently I started again to collect warhammer40k, so I will upload here photos of the miniatures we paint and the games we play.

1. My first tyranids


When I was a child I had a lot of warhammers, but all of them where fantasy (lizardmen, vampires, skavens...). I mainly played with my brother, who also had a lot of them. But I always wanted to dive into the 40k world, although I didn't have friends to play with, so I never did.

After a lot of years (I think more than 10 years without painting) I somehow managed to convince my friends to give a try on 40k. They had never played warhammers before and were shocked by the complexity of the rules and how interesting and funny is the game. I had a bad time deciding the race, because I usually like a lot. In this case my main options were necrons, orks, eldars and what I finally chose: Tyranids.

I really like aliens, and I am enjoying painting them because I really found a color pattern I like (this turquoise in the armor along with the palid flesh). I will upload more photos when I finish the squadrons, and comment what were the more difficult parts. For now on, the wings of the gargoyles are the most difficult thing I faced.
