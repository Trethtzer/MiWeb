I decided a few years ago that I will start learning a new language every 5 years, and for now I kept my promise.

I will point out here all the resources I found that were useful to me, along with some notes about curious things I found when learning this languages.

1. A dynamic way to learn Korean.


I really like to be efficient and to learn languages in a way that sticks into my memory. That is why I have been trying different methods and ways to learn korean during this five years.

Today I want to speak about a website and youtube channel that I found, which in fact has different programs for each language they offer, though I will speak only for the korean as it is the one I have been trying so far.

Here you can access through the links to the website and youtube channel:

  • https://www.koreanclass101.com
    • Link to the website where you can navigate through the variety of content they have. There is also an mobile app and they offer a variety of programs depending on how much money do you want to invest (from 0 to maybe around 50 a month).
  • Youtube channel
    • Here you have the youtube channel, where they post content weekly.


I am going to speak a bit more about the Youtube channel. Here you can find some videos advertising the website and all the content they offer, but there are also videos to learn certain grammars or practice some vocabulary.
There is also streams in youtube, which will be later posted as videos. In those streams you can interact with the teacher and get a better understanding of whatever you are studying at the moment.
Finally, there is a TV channel in youtube, which is looping through several lessons. Once you know the basics, it could be useful to see it on background while doing other things. You may discover new grammars / vocabulary that can be useful in your daily life.
